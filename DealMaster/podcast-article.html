By Nathan Anibaba
April 7, 2019

This week on the podcast, I have an extra special guest and you are in for a treat. His name is Jeb Blount. If you want to know the latest thinking in B2B sales from a practitioner at the top of his field then you’re going to want to stick around and listen to the show.

He’s written 9 wonderful books. If you haven’t read Fanatical prospecting and Sales EQ and you’re wondering why you aren’t winning more new business, I suggest you pick up a copy.
